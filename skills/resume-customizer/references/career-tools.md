### Negotiation principles (non-negotiable)

1. **Never give a number first** — always ask for their range first
2. **When pressed:** Give a range, not a single number
3. **Always negotiate:** Base, signing, equity, title
4. **Leverage:** Multiple processes = stronger position (only mention if true)
5. **Illiquid equity argument (if applicable):** "My current equity is illiquid - I'd need liquid compensation or publicly-traded equity to offset"

|------|-------------|-------|

| Signing bonus | Yes | Cover unvested forfeit |

## COUNTER-OFFER HANDLING

### Context

If your current manager has been told you're leaving, a counter-offer is possible. Plan the response before you receive one.

### If your current company counters

**Typical counter:**

- 20-30% raise
- Title bump
- Expanded scope
- Retention bonus

**How to evaluate:**

1. Why were you looking in the first place? (Stagnation? Ceiling? Scope?)
2. Does the counter fix the root cause? (Usually not — same org, same ceiling)
3. Statistic worth knowing: ~80% of people who accept counter-offers leave within 18 months

### Recommended response

```
"I appreciate the counter-offer and my time at {{current company}}. However, my
decision to move was about {{growth / scope / new challenges}}, not compensation.
The company I'm joining offers {{specific thing}} that addresses what I'm
looking for. I've made my commitment and will honor it."
```

### Notice-period negotiation

- Current: {{Your notice period}}
- Options:
    - Buyout by new company (ask them for a signing bonus to offset)
    - Early release by current company (use accumulated goodwill)
    - Unused PTO offset
- Ask new company: "Would you consider a signing bonus to offset notice-buyout costs?"

## 90-DAY PLANS (COMPANY-TYPE SPECIFIC)

Pick the matching template when prepping for interviews where a 90-day plan is requested.

### For AI Companies

```
DAYS 1-30: IMMERSE
- Shadow 5 customer / user calls to understand AI use cases
- Map current model capabilities vs product gaps
- Build relationship with ML / Research leads
- Ship one small AI feature to understand the deployment pipeline

DAYS 31-60: IDENTIFY
- Present "3 opportunities" doc to leadership
- Own one 0-to-1 AI feature end-to-end
- Establish product-ML collaboration rhythm
- Define success metrics for AI features (not just usage — value delivery)

DAYS 61-90: ACCELERATE
- Launch identified feature with measurable impact
- Present product strategy recommendations to exec team
- Build hiring plan for team expansion
- Establish quarterly OKR framework
```

### For Consumer Scale Companies

```
DAYS 1-30: IMMERSE
- Deep-dive into growth metrics (DAU, retention, engagement)
- Map user journey and drop-off points
- Meet 10 users directly (not just data)
- Understand experimentation infrastructure

DAYS 31-60: IDENTIFY
- Run 3 A/B tests on high-impact areas
- Present "quick wins" roadmap to leadership
- Own one growth initiative end-to-end
- Build cross-functional relationships (Growth, Data, Engineering)

DAYS 61-90: ACCELERATE
- Show measurable metric improvement in owned area
- Present 6-month product strategy
- Establish experimentation cadence (weekly shipping)
- Define team structure needs
```

### For Enterprise / B2B

```
DAYS 1-30: IMMERSE
- Shadow 5 enterprise sales calls
- Map current product gaps from customer feedback
- Understand enterprise procurement and compliance needs
- Build relationships with Sales, CS, and Engineering leads

DAYS 31-60: IDENTIFY
- Present customer-validated roadmap priorities
- Own one customer-requested feature
- Establish product-sales feedback loop
- Define success metrics (NRR, adoption, CSAT)

DAYS 61-90: ACCELERATE
- Ship feature with customer validation
- Present product strategy to leadership
- Establish quarterly planning with sales alignment
- Build hiring plan if applicable
```

### For Fintech

```
DAYS 1-30: IMMERSE
- Understand payment / transaction flows end-to-end
- Map compliance requirements (regional regulators, PCI-DSS, etc.)
- Meet with risk, compliance, and ops teams
- Understand unit economics (take rate, fraud rate, processing margin)

DAYS 31-60: IDENTIFY
- Present "payment success improvement" or "TPV growth" roadmap
- Own one conversion / reliability initiative
- Establish metrics framework (TPV, success rate, latency)
- Build relationships with bank / partner teams

DAYS 61-90: ACCELERATE
- Show measurable improvement in success rate or TPV
- Present product strategy to leadership
- Establish operational excellence cadence
- Define team and infrastructure needs
```

### For Telcos

```
DAYS 1-30: IMMERSE
- Map telco partnership landscape and integration patterns
- Understand existing product and revenue models
- Meet key telco relationship stakeholders
- Assess product gaps and partner feedback

DAYS 31-60: IDENTIFY
- Identify partnership expansion opportunities
- Understand telco-specific compliance requirements
- Assess distribution model optimization
- Build relationships with partner product teams

DAYS 61-90: ACCELERATE
- Propose new partnership or deeper integration
- Build business case with revenue impact
- Establish partnership product roadmap
- Define success metrics (ARPU, deployment, engagement)
```
