## ATS COMPLIANCE REMINDERS

### Character rules

- Use "USD" / "EUR" not currency symbols
- Use "to" not arrow (→)
- Use "-" not em-dash (—)

### Never say

- "Led the platform" — lead WHAT specifically?
- "Owner of Revenue Platform" — without ARR attribution it's vague
- "0 to 1 product" — only used when literally first PM, not when joining existing surface

### Always say

- "Owned [surface] including [scope]"
- "Drove [metric] through [action]"
- "Built [thing] that achieved [verified metric]"
