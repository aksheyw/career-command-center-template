# Director of Product, AI Platform
**Northstar AI · Remote (US) · Full-time · Posted 2026-05-08**

Northstar AI is a Series B AI infrastructure company building the deployment + observability layer for enterprise LLM applications. Our customers ship production AI features at Fortune 500 scale; we make that fast, debuggable, and safe.

We're hiring a Director of Product for the AI Platform — the surface that exposes our LLM routing, eval harness, and inference observability to customer teams. You'll own roadmap, GTM partnership with sales/marketing, and a team of 4-6 PMs.

## What you'll do

- Own end-to-end product strategy for the AI Platform surface ($18M ARR today, $50M target by EOY 2026)
- Build and scale a PM team across 3 sub-surfaces: Routing, Evaluation, Observability
- Partner with engineering leadership on the platform-vs-feature decisions that compound across every customer integration
- Lead pricing strategy for usage-based AI infrastructure (per-token + per-request hybrid)
- Co-own enterprise partnerships with cloud and data-platform vendors (3-5 strategic deals/year)
- Drive measurable ARR attribution on every surface you ship — no surface launches without an ARR target and a measurement plan
- Translate ambiguous enterprise customer requirements into shipped surfaces; protect platform integrity from one-off customer asks

## What we're looking for

- 8+ years product management; 2+ years at Director level
- B2B SaaS background with revenue infrastructure or developer tooling experience
- Demonstrated track record shipping AI/ML features end-to-end (own PRD + model selection + GTM, not just feature spec)
- Technical depth — comfortable in API contracts, latency tradeoffs, deployment architecture
- Pricing/packaging chops for usage-based SKUs (per-token, per-request, or per-event)
- Hands-on PM hiring + people leadership; built and grew a team across multiple surfaces
- Strong written communication; can write a PRD that holds up under engineering scrutiny

## Bonus

- Experience with audit firm or systems integrator partnerships
- Engineering background (SWE → PM transition)
- Side projects shipping real AI products

## Compensation

- Base: $280-320K USD
- Equity: 0.15-0.30% (4-year vest, 1-year cliff)
- Annual bonus: up to 25% of base, tied to surface-level ARR attainment
- Remote-friendly (US); quarterly travel to SF HQ

## How we evaluate

5 rounds: recruiter → hiring manager (Head of Product) → case study (live, 60 min) → panel (eng + sales + CS leads) → CEO/CTO final. Total ~12-15 hours candidate time.
